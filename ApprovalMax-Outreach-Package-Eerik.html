The Three Growth Feedback Loops

Three compounding loops that turn ApprovalMax's acquisition system into one that gets stronger over time.

Loop 1

Paid Acquisition → Customer Story → Better Conversion

Paid acquisition brings new finance teams into free trial. Trial activation sequence converts trials to paid customers. Paid customers who experience real outcomes become case studies. Case studies go back onto landing pages as social proof. Better social proof improves trial conversion rates. Higher conversion rates make paid acquisition more efficient. The loop compounds.

Loop 2

Accounting Partner Referral → Review Growth

Accounting partners recommend ApprovalMax to clients. Referred clients start trials with higher intent. Higher-intent trials convert to paid at higher rates. Happy paid customers leave five-star reviews on the Xero App Store and Google My Business. More positive reviews attract more accounting partners. A targeted LinkedIn Ads campaign for accounting firms accelerates the entry point of this loop. More partners. More referrals. More reviews.

Loop 3

Test, Measure, Learn, Scale
